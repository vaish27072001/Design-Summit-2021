ABOUT US


Google Developer Student Club JECRC University aims to proffer a major contribution to the design community all over India by boosting up the development and by providing support to the emerging designers in India. We also aim to guide those students who are interested in graphics and web designing and wish to pursue their career in this domain professionally, give brief learning and understanding of this domain.

Also, we will try to provoke interest in graphic designing in the minds of students who do not know much about this domain.


Brainstorming talk shows and charismatic panel discussions from experts.


Hands on sessions and workshop from Prominent Industry Designers.


The first Design Summit organised in Rajasthan, India.
